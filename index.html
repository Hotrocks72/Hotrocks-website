About the Band

Hot Rocks brings the legendary energy of The Rolling Stones to life with a high-energy, authentic tribute show. From the iconic hits to deep-cut fan favorites, Hot Rocks delivers a concert experience that captures the spirit, sound, and swagger of the world’s greatest rock and roll band.
